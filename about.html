About Me

I work at the intersection of financial crime investigations, risk systems, and practical AI. My focus is building investigator-first tools that reduce complexity and support confident decision making in environments where the stakes are real.

How My Experience Shaped My Work

I came to this work through financial crime investigations and regulatory environments where decisions have to hold up long after they are made.

Across investigations, remediation efforts, and compliance work, I spent years inside systems designed to detect risk at scale but not to support the people responsible for understanding it. Alerts piled up. Context lived in too many places. Investigators were expected to move fast while defending every decision.

That gap is what pulled me toward building investigator first tools. I focus on practical AI and structured workflows that surface what matters earlier, preserve reasoning, and help investigators stay confident in the decisions they are accountable for.
