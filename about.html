About Me

I joined PwC in 2019 as an analyst, bringing more than 14 years of prior experience across insurance, healthcare, online education, client management, and large national banks.

That background shaped how I think about systems ‚Äî not in theory, but in practice.

Before working directly in financial crime, my work focused on cross-functional delivery, operational readiness, and regulatory support. I saw firsthand how policies translate into execution, and where systems either support people or create friction.
